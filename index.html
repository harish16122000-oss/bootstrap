About us

Lingam Bikes was founded with a clear vision — to deliver reliable, stylish, and performance-driven motorcycles for everyday riders. The journey began with a passion for engineering, innovation, and a deep understanding of Indian road conditions. Lingam Bikes is a passionate two-wheeler brand dedicated to delivering performance, style, and reliability. Founded with the vision of creating bikes that match modern lifestyles, Lingam Bikes focuses on quality engineering, innovative design, and rider satisfaction.
